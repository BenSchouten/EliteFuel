# EliteFuel Submission Notes

## 1-Sentence Pitch

EliteFuel is an AI-powered youth sports nutrition platform that turns team schedules, meal photos, hydration trends, and athlete context into practical support for clubs, staff, athletes, and parents.

## 3-Sentence Project Description

EliteFuel helps youth sports clubs offer nutrition support without needing a full-time dietitian on every team. Athletes and parents can log meals, correct AI interpretation, view hydration trends, and get schedule-aware fueling guidance, while staff use roster check-ins and quick reviews to spot who needs a practical follow-up. Club admins manage teams, rosters, staff assignments, schedules, and a club meal library in one role-aware workspace.

## What Makes It Impressive

- Full role-aware SaaS MVP across Club Admin, Staff, Athlete, and Parent experiences.
- AI meal photo feedback is grounded in visible components and user corrections instead of generic advice.
- Team schedule context flows into athlete, parent, staff, and meal feedback surfaces.
- Youth-safe product boundaries avoid weight-loss, diagnosis, supplement advice, and body-shaming.
- Demo seed data shows realistic cross-platform interactions: roster status, follow-ups, meals, hydration, schedules, and library examples.

## Suggested 3-Minute Demo Script

1. **Club Admin:** Open Club admin, show setup readiness, Team roster with varied account states, Team schedule with planned dates, and Club meal library.
2. **Staff:** Sign in as Jordan, show Staff Operations, switch assigned teams, toggle filters, and open a quick athlete review to show reason/action/evidence.
3. **Athlete:** Sign in as Maya, show Athlete overview, Meals, AI meal feedback, correction/add-details flow, hydration trend, schedule, and library examples.
4. **Parent:** Sign in as Morgan, show linked-child selector, parent-friendly meal/hydration/schedule support, and profile/food safety update access.
5. **Close:** Emphasize that EliteFuel is club-scoped, youth-safe, and built around practical workflows instead of a public social feed.

## Suggested Handshake Submission Description

EliteFuel is a production-minded local MVP for AI-powered youth sports nutrition support. It combines Next.js, TypeScript, Prisma, PostgreSQL, NextAuth, Tailwind, and OpenAI text/vision APIs to create role-aware workflows for club admins, staff, athletes, and parents. The app’s flagship feature is grounded AI meal photo feedback that uses visible meal components, user corrections, timing context, athlete goals, and team schedules to generate practical fueling recommendations while keeping youth safety and privacy boundaries clear.
